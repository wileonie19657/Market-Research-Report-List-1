<p><p>The flexible rubber insulation market comprises various types based on material composition. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) offers excellent resistance to oils and fuels, making it suitable for industrial applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is highly durable and weather-resistant, ideal for outdoor and HVAC applications. Chloroprene rubber is known for its resilience and thermal stability, utilized in commercial setting. Other materials may include silicone and neoprene, catering to specialized requirements in diverse sectors like automotive, construction, and HVAC.</p></p>
