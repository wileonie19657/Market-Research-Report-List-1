<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber jumper market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode fibers utilize a single light path, allowing for longer transmission distances and higher bandwidth, making them ideal for long-haul applications. In contrast, multimode fibers have multiple light paths, enabling higher data rates over short distances, suitable for local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct needs based on distance, bandwidth, and application requirements, catering to various communication infrastructure demands.</p></p>
