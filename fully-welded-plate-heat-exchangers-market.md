<p><p>Fully welded plate heat exchangers encompass two primary market types: plate-and-block heat exchangers and plate-and-shell heat exchangers. Plate-and-block HE combines welded plates and block structures, maximizing thermal efficiency and minimizing pressure drops, making it ideal for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, plate-and-shell HE features a combination of plate heat exchanger technology and a cylindrical shell, allowing for higher pressure and temperature applications, thereby enhancing durability and versatility in various industrial processes.</p></p>
