<p><p>The baited insect traps market comprises several key types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed devices that attract and contain insects, offering targeted pest control. Granular baits consist of small particles spread across infested areas, appealing to insects while being safe for use around pets and humans. Gel baits are viscous formulations applied in small amounts, delivering effective pest control by attracting insects and enabling easy application in hard-to-reach spaces.</p></p>
