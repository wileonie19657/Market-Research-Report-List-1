<p><strong>The B-type Ultrasound Diagnostic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B-type ultrasound diagnostic systems are categorized into three primary types based on scanning methods: fanshaped, linear, and mixed type. Fanshaped scanning uses a wide-angle beam to capture images over a broad area, ideal for organs like the heart. Linear scanning employs parallel lines to provide high-resolution images, making it effective for vascular and superficial structures. Mixed type combines both approaches, leveraging the strengths of each to offer versatile imaging capabilities suitable for various diagnostic applications in healthcare.</p></p>
