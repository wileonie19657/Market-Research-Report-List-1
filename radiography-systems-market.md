<p><p>The radiography systems market is primarily categorized into digital and analog types. Digital radiography utilizes advanced sensor technology to capture images, providing immediate, high-resolution results and facilitating enhanced image analysis and storage. This method improves workflow and reduces exposure to radiation. In contrast, analog radiography relies on traditional film-based systems, producing images through X-ray film that requires chemical processing. While generally more cost-effective, analog systems may have longer processing times and less efficient image management compared to their digital counterparts.</p></p>
