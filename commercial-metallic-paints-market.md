<p><strong>The Commercial Metallic Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial metallic paints market is divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints utilize organic solvents as their primary carrier, offering excellent durability and finish. They are ideal for high-performance applications but emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based metallic paints use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC levels. They provide good adhesion and color retention, making them suitable for diverse applications while facilitating easier cleanup and safer handling.</p></p>
