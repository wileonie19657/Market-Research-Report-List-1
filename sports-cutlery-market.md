<p><p>The sports cutlery market can be categorized into two main types: folding knives and fixed blade knives. Folding knives are compact and portable, featuring a hinge mechanism that allows the blade to fold into the handle, making them ideal for outdoor activities requiring convenience and safety. Fixed blade knives, on the other hand, have a blade that is permanently fixed in place, offering greater strength and durability, making them suitable for tasks that demand heavy-duty performance, such as camping and hunting.</p></p>
