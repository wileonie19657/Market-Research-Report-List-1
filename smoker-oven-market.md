<p><p>The smoker oven market is primarily divided into two types: gas and electric. In contrast, electric smoker ovens rely on electricity to generate heat, providing consistent temperatures and easier temperature control, making them suitable for indoor use. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with gas appealing to traditional outdoor cooking enthusiasts and electric attracting those seeking convenience and reliability.</p></p>
