<p><ul><li>CTS (Conversion Track System)</li><li>TTS (Trailed Track System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber track market for agricultural machinery includes two main types: Conversion Track System (CTS) and Trailed Track System (TTS). CTS involves retrofitting wheeled equipment with rubber tracks, enhancing traction and reducing soil compaction. In contrast, TTS features machinery specifically designed with mounted tracks that provide stability and better weight distribution. Both systems improve performance in diverse terrains, ensuring efficient operations while minimizing environmental impact, making them essential for modern agricultural practices.</p></p>
