<p><strong>The Rapid Medical Diagnostic Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow-Through</li><li>Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Medical Diagnostic Devices market comprises several types, including lateral flow assays, agglutination assays, flow-through assays, and biosensors. Lateral flow assays use capillary action for quick results, typically in point-of-care settings. Agglutination assays involve the clumping of particles in response to an antigen, useful for various tests. Flow-through assays utilize a membrane for sample processing, providing reliable results. Biosensors leverage biological components to detect analytes, offering real-time monitoring and high sensitivity in diagnostics.</p></p>
