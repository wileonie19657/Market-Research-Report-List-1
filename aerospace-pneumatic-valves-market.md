<p><p>The aerospace pneumatic valves market consists of various types that control airflow and pressure in aircraft systems. Poppet valves utilize a disc to seal the flow, ensuring reliable operation. Pilot valves act as control devices, regulating larger valves. Flapper-nozzle valves use a flexible flapper to direct airflow, optimizing performance. Ball and plug valves feature rotating elements for efficient flow control. Other types include spool and solenoid valves, catering to specialized applications in aerospace systems, enhancing safety and functionality.</p></p>
