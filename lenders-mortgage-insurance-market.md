<p><strong>The Lenders Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-paid (BPMI)</li><li>Lender-paid (LPMI)</li><li>Single Premium</li><li>Split Premium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) provides protection to lenders against borrower default. There are several market types: Borrower-Paid Mortgage Insurance (BPMI), where borrowers pay the insurance premium, often included in monthly payments; Lender-Paid Mortgage Insurance (LPMI), where lenders cover the cost, typically resulting in a higher interest rate; Single Premium, which is a one-time upfront payment; and Split Premium, where part is paid upfront and the remaining is included in monthly payments. Each option varies in cost and impact on borrower payments.</p></p>
