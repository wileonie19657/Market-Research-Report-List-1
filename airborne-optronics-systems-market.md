<p><p>The Airborne Optronics Systems market is categorized into multispectral and hyperspectral segments. Multispectral systems capture data across a limited range of wavelengths, allowing for effective analysis of surface materials and features. They are commonly used in applications like agriculture and surveillance. Hyperspectral systems, on the other hand, collect data across a much wider spectrum, providing detailed information on material composition, enabling precise identification and analysis. This enhanced capability makes hyperspectral systems valuable in environmental monitoring and defense applications.</p></p>
