<p><strong>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS)</li><li>Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS Image Sensor market is mainly divided into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS). PPS utilizes a simpler design with less sensitivity and performance, primarily found in lower-end applications. In contrast, APS features individual transistors for each pixel, enhancing image quality, sensitivity, and speed, making it suitable for advanced applications like smartphones and professional cameras. This distinction underlines the technological evolution in image capture, catering to varying market demands for performance and cost efficiency.</p></p>
