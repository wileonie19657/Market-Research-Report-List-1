<p><p>The spray pump market primarily consists of diaphragm and centrifugal pumps, each serving distinct applications. Diaphragm pumps are known for their ability to handle viscous liquids and provide precise flow control, making them ideal for agricultural and chemical spraying. In contrast, centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move liquids and are typically used for high flow rates in applications like irrigation and industrial spraying. Both types are essential in optimizing efficiency and effectiveness in their respective sectors.</p></p>
