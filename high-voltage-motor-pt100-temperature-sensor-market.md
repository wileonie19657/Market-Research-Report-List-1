<p><p>The High Voltage Motor Pt100 Temperature Sensor market is divided into two main types: Bearing Type and Stator Embedded Type. Bearing Type sensors are installed in the motor's bearings to monitor their temperature, ensuring optimal lubrication and preventing overheating. In contrast, Stator Embedded Type sensors are integrated directly into the stator windings, providing accurate temperature readings of the windings during operation. Both types are crucial for enhancing motor reliability, efficiency, and longevity by enabling timely maintenance interventions.</p></p>
