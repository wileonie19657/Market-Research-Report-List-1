<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) market includes DC and AC charging types. DC charging provides high-power, fast charging directly to the vehicle's battery, ideal for public stations and long-distance travel. AC charging delivers lower power, typically used for home or workplace charging, requiring the vehicle's onboard charger to convert AC to DC. Both types cater to different needs: DC for quick top-ups and AC for overnight or extended charging, supporting the growing EV infrastructure and diverse user requirements.</p></p>
