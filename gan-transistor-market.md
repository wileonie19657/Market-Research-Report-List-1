<p><p>The GaN transistor market comprises primarily two types: GaN on SiC (Silicon Carbide) and GaN on Si (Silicon). GaN on SiC offers high efficiency and thermal performance, making it suitable for demanding applications like power amplifiers and high-frequency devices, often used in RF and aerospace sectors. Conversely, GaN on Si is more cost-effective, suitable for consumer electronics and lower-power applications, favoring mass production. Both technologies provide unique advantages, catering to different industry needs and market segments.</p></p>
