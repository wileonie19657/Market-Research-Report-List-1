<p><p>Carbon copy paper, also known as carbonless paper, is used to create duplicate copies of documents without the need for carbon paper. This type of paper incorporates micro-encapsulated dye or ink that reacts upon pressure, transferring imprints to a second sheet placed underneath. Technology advancements in printing methods and the introduction of high-quality products to enhance user experience are also shaping the market.
