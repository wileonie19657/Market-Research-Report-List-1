<p><p>The antibody market is categorized based on the type of immunoglobulins: IgM, IgG, IgA, and others. IgM antibodies are primarily involved in the early immune response, while IgG antibodies provide long-term immunity and are the most abundant in serum. IgA is crucial for mucosal immunity, found in secretions like saliva and tears. Other antibodies, including IgE and IgD, play roles in allergy and immune regulation. Each type serves distinct functions in diagnostics and therapeutics, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
