<p><p>Furnace soldering services encompass three market types based on solder material properties: soft state, semi-hard state, and hard state. </p><p>Soft state soldering produces joints that remain flexible, ideal for applications with thermal expansion variations. Semi-hard state soldering combines flexibility with some rigidity, suitable for moderate stress conditions. Hard state soldering creates rigid joints, offering the highest strength and durability for demanding applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, optimizing performance based on mechanical and thermal requirements.</p></p>
