<p><ul><li>Rubber Sleeve</li><li>Magnetic Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerator door gaskets market is primarily categorized into two types: rubber sleeve gaskets and magnetic strip gaskets. Rubber sleeve gaskets are flexible and create a tight seal, ensuring energy efficiency and temperature retention. They are commonly used due to their durability and adaptability to different door designs. Magnetic strip gaskets, on the other hand, utilize embedded magnets to provide a secure closure, enhancing airtightness while offering ease of installation. Both types play a crucial role in reducing energy costs and maintaining food freshness.</p></p>
