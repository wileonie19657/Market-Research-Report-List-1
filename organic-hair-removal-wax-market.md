<p><p>The organic hair removal wax market is primarily divided into two types: soft wax and hard wax. In contrast, hard wax is thicker, applied directly to the skin, and hardens upon cooling, allowing it to be pulled off without strips, making it ideal for sensitive areas like the bikini line and underarms. Both types cater to various consumer preferences for hair removal solutions.</p></p>
