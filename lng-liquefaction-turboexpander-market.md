<p><p>The LNG liquefaction turboexpander market comprises three main types: centrifugal turboexpanders, axial-flow turboexpanders, and others. Axial-flow turboexpanders operate through a series of rotating blades, making them suitable for lower-capacity settings with specific flow requirements.
