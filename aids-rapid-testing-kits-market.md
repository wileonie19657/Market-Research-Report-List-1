<p><p>AIDS rapid testing kits are essential tools for the quick diagnosis of HIV, offering two primary testing types: blood tests and saliva tests. Blood tests typically require a small sample from a fingertip or vein, providing results usually within 15-30 minutes, and are highly accurate. Saliva tests, on the other hand, use oral fluid samples, making them less invasive and easier to administer, though slightly less sensitive. Both types cater to different needs, enhancing accessibility and timely diagnosis in various settings.</p></p>
