<p><p>The aviation actuators market is primarily categorized into three types: electromechanical actuators, hydraulic actuators, and others. Electromechanical actuators convert electrical energy into mechanical motion, often used for precise movements in flight controls. Hydraulic actuators utilize pressurized fluid to create motion and are favored for their strength and reliability, particularly in larger aircraft systems. The "others" category includes pneumatic actuators and other specialized devices that support various applications within the aviation sector, enhancing overall efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
