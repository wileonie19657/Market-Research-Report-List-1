<p><strong>The Wireless VOC Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless VOC monitor market primarily includes two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID monitors use ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds in the air, providing real-time detection and measurement of low concentration VOCs. In contrast, MOS sensors operate by measuring conductivity changes in a metal-oxide layer upon gas exposure, making them suitable for detecting a broad range of VOCs. Both technologies offer wireless capabilities, enhancing ease of monitoring in various environments.</p></p>
