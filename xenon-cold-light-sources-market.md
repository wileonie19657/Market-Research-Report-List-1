<p><p>Xenon cold light sources are classified into two primary market types: long arc and short arc. Long arc xenon sources utilize an extended arc length to produce a broader, more uniform light distribution, making them ideal for high-intensity applications like cinema and surgical lighting. In contrast, short arc xenon sources feature a compact arc length, delivering intense beams with higher brightness, suited for projectors and high-resolution displays. Both types are valued for their excellent color rendering and efficiency in various lighting applications.</p></p>
