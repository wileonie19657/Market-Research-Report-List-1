<p><p>The seat belt retractor market is segmented into three types based on the number of plug connectors. Single Stage (1-Stage) retractors feature a single plug connector, offering basic functionality and simplicity. Dual Stage (2-Stage) retractors utilize two plugs, allowing for adjustable tension and improved safety features. Triple Stage (3-Stage) retractors come with three plug connectors, providing advanced tension management and enhanced restraint systems for optimal passenger protection. Each stage increases complexity and safety, catering to varying vehicle requirements.</p></p>
