<p><strong>The Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically-steered Antenna</li><li>Mechanically-steered Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat panel antennas market is primarily categorized into electronically-steered and mechanically-steered antennas. Electronically-steered antennas utilize phased array technology to steer beams rapidly and precisely without physical movement, offering advantages like quick response time and compact design, often used in military and communication applications. In contrast, mechanically-steered antennas rely on physical movement to change their orientation, which may result in slower response times but can provide cost-effective solutions for applications like satellite communications and broadcasting.</p></p>
