<p><p>The Zika virus testing market is divided into two primary types: molecular tests and serologic tests. Molecular tests, such as PCR, detect the virus's genetic material in bodily fluids, providing quick and accurate results during active infections. In contrast, serologic tests identify antibodies produced in response to the virus, indicating past infections. Both testing types are essential for diagnosing Zika infections, monitoring outbreaks, and guiding public health responses, each serving distinct roles in Zika virus management.</p></p>
