<p><strong>The Air Admittance Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zero Pressure</li><li>Positive Pressure</li><li>Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air Admittance Valves (AAVs) are classified based on the pressure conditions they operate under. Zero Pressure AAVs function without any pressure difference, allowing air to enter drainage systems to prevent siphoning. Positive Pressure AAVs are designed to maintain airflow under pressurized conditions, ensuring proper venting during high flow rates. Negative Pressure AAVs handle situations where drainage systems experience vacuum conditions, effectively maintaining balance while preventing backflow of sewer gases. Each type is crucial for optimizing drainage efficiency and preventing plumbing issues.</p></p>
