<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Application-specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Standard-cell Based</li><li>Gate-array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application-specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) are tailored for specific applications, with the market categorized into three main types. Full-custom design involves creating circuits from scratch for optimal performance and efficiency, allowing for complete flexibility but requiring more time and cost. Standard-cell based ASICs utilize pre-designed cells to speed up development, balancing customization and efficiency. Gate-array based ASICs offer a middle ground, using a pre-fabricated array that can be configured post-manufacturing, providing a quicker and often less costly solution than full-custom designs.</p></p>
