<p><strong>The Digital Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital gas meter market encompasses two main types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems facilitate remote data collection, allowing utilities to gather usage information at predetermined intervals without manual readings. In contrast, AMI provides a more integrated solution, enabling two-way communication between the meter and utility, offering real-time data, enhanced monitoring, and control capabilities. Both technologies aim to increase efficiency, accuracy, and customer engagement in gas consumption tracking and management.</p></p>
