<p><p>Non-destructive testing (NDT) radiography equipment encompasses various technologies for inspecting materials without causing damage. X-ray systems utilize high-energy photons to reveal internal structures, commonly used in industrial applications and welding inspections. Gamma ray equipment employs radioactive isotopes, effective for thick materials and ensuring safety in hazardous environments. Neutron ray systems use neutron radiation, ideal for detecting defects in light metals and hydrogenous materials. Each type provides unique benefits, catering to diverse industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and infrastructure.</p></p>
