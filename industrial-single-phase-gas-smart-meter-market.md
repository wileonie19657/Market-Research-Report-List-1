<p><p>The Industrial Single Phase Gas Smart Meter market is divided into two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters use integrated circuit technology for secure transactions, allowing users to easily recharge and manage their gas usage through prepaid cards. In contrast, Non-IC Card meters operate on traditional billing systems, often relying on manual readings or postpaid arrangements. Both types cater to businesses seeking efficient gas consumption management but differ in functionality and convenience based on payment methods.</p></p>
