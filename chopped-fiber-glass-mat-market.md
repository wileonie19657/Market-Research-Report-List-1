<p><p>The chopped fiberglass mat market is categorized into three main types: C-Glass, HR-Glass, and SiO2-Glass. C-Glass is durable and cost-effective, making it ideal for general applications. HR-Glass, or high-performance glass, offers superior strength and heat resistance, suitable for specialized projects. SiO2-Glass, primarily composed of silica, is known for its high-temperature resistance and low thermal expansion, commonly utilized in high-tech industries. Each type fulfills specific needs, enhancing the versatility of fiberglass applications in various sectors.</p></p>
