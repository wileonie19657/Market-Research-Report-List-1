<p><ul><li>Enclosed</li><li>Clamp-On</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orbital weld heads market is categorized into two main types: enclosed and clamp-on. Enclosed weld heads are designed to provide a protective casing around the welding operation, ensuring stability and shielding the weld from contaminants. This type is ideal for high-purity environments. Clamp-on weld heads, on the other hand, are versatile and can be easily attached to various pipe diameters, allowing for quick setups and adjustments. Both types cater to different industrial applications, enhancing weld quality and efficiency.</p></p>
