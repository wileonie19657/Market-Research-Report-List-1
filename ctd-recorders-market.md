<p><strong>The CTD Recorders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Profiling CTD</li><li>Moored CTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CTD recorders, essential for measuring conductivity, temperature, and depth in aquatic environments, are categorized mainly into two market types: profiling CTD and moored CTD systems. Profiling CTDs are deployed from vessels and descend through the water column, providing detailed data across various depths. In contrast, moored CTDs are fixed at a specific location, continuously collecting data over extended periods, making them ideal for monitoring environmental changes and long-term studies in marine ecosystems.</p></p>
