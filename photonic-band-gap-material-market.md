<p><p>The photonic band-gap material market is categorized into three types: 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D photonic crystals. </p><p>1-D photonic crystals consist of alternating layers of different materials, creating one-dimensional band gaps for controlling light propagation. </p><p>2-D photonic crystals feature a periodic arrangement of materials in two dimensions, allowing for advanced manipulation of light through planar structures. </p><p>3-D photonic crystals provide a three-dimensional periodic structure, offering more extensive light control and broader applications in telecommunications, sensors, and optoelectronics.</p></p>
