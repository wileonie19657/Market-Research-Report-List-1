<p><p>The heating cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single guide type and double guide type. Single guide type heating cables consist of a single conductor and are ideal for straightforward applications requiring minimalist installation and effective heating. In contrast, double guide type heating cables feature a dual conductor design, allowing for enhanced heating performance and energy efficiency, making them suitable for more complex and demanding heating tasks. Both types cater to various industrial and residential heating needs.</p></p>
