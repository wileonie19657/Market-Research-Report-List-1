<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial exhaust fans market comprises mainly two types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to increase air pressure, making them ideal for applications requiring high air resistance, such as industrial settings. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient ventilation for larger areas like warehouses or kitchens. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining air quality and temperature control in commercial environments, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
