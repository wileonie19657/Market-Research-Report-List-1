<p><ul><li>Phase 1</li><li>Phase 2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs) are classified into two main market types: Phase 1 and Phase 2. Phase 1 assessments focus on identifying potential environmental liabilities by evaluating a property's history, usage, and surrounding environment without physical testing. If potential contamination is identified, Phase 2 assessments involve more in-depth investigation, including soil and groundwater testing to quantify the level of contamination. Together, these assessments help inform buyers, lenders, and developers of risks associated with real estate transactions.</p></p>
