<p><p>The Electronic Component Screening Machine Gripper market features two primary types: double-joint and multi-joint grippers. Double-joint grippers utilize two rotating joints to provide enhanced flexibility and reach, making them suitable for handling larger or awkwardly shaped components. In contrast, multi-joint grippers employ multiple flexible joints, allowing for more intricate movements and adaptability in complex screening tasks. Both types aim to improve efficiency and precision in the handling and placement of electronic components during manufacturing and testing processes.</p></p>
