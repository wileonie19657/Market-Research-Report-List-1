<p><p>The matte photo paper market encompasses two primary types: silver halide photo paper and inkjet photo paper. Silver halide photo paper is traditionally used in film photography, offering vibrant color reproduction and deep blacks, making it ideal for professional prints. Inkjet photo paper, on the other hand, is designed for digital printing, featuring coatings that enhance ink absorption and drying time. Both types cater to different printing technologies, emphasizing quality, durability, and specific aesthetic finishes for photographers and artists.</p></p>
