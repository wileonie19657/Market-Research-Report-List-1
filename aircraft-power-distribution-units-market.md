<p><strong>The Aircraft Power Distribution Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Power Distribution Unit</li><li>Secondary Power Distribution Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft power distribution units market is segmented into primary and secondary power distribution units. Primary power distribution units manage the initial distribution of power from the aircraft's generators to various systems, ensuring efficient energy supply and load management. In contrast, secondary power distribution units distribute power from the primary units to specific aircraft systems and components, providing localized control and redundancy. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable and safe electrical power distribution throughout the aircraft.</p></p>
