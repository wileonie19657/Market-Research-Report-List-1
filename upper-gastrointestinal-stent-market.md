<p><p>The Upper Gastrointestinal Stent market encompasses various types designed for specific anatomical regions and conditions. Esophageal stents aid in managing strictures or obstructions in the esophagus. Duodenal stents are utilized for patients with duodenal obstructions, often due to tumors. Colonic stents assist in treating colonic blockages, providing a minimally invasive solution. Biliary stents relieve bile duct obstructions, improving bile flow, while pancreatic stents facilitate drainage in cases of pancreatitis or pancreatic duct strictures, enhancing therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
