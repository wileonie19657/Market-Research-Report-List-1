<p><p>The lithium battery manufacturing machinery market comprises three main segments: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves preparing raw materials, ensuring purity and proper handling. Cell assembly focuses on constructing the battery components, including electrode stacking, coating, and electrolyte filling, essential for performance and safety. Post-processing encompasses final procedures like formation, testing, and packaging, ensuring the batteries meet quality standards. Each segment is critical for efficient production and reliability of lithium-ion batteries used in various applications.</p></p>
