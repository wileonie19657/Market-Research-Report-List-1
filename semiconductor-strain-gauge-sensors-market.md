<p><p>Semiconductor strain gauge sensors are critical in measuring mechanical deformation, available mainly in two market types: naked gauges and backed gauges. Naked gauges consist of a thin, sensitive sensing element directly exposed to strain, providing high sensitivity and precision. In contrast, backed gauges feature a protective layer that offers enhanced durability and stability, making them suitable for harsher environments. Both types find applications across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and structural health monitoring, ensuring accurate strain measurements under different conditions.</p></p>
