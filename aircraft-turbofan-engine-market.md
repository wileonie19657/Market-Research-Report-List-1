<p><strong>The Aircraft Turbofan Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Bypass Type</li><li>Low-Bypass Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft turbofan engine market is categorized into high-bypass and low-bypass types based on the bypass ratio, which is the amount of air bypassing the engine core compared to the air passing through it. High-bypass turbofans, commonly used in commercial aviation, offer greater fuel efficiency and quieter operation. Low-bypass engines, often found in military and older commercial aircraft, provide higher thrust and are better suited for supersonic speeds. Both types cater to specific performance and operational needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
