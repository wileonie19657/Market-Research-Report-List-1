<p><p>The XPS geofoams market is categorized into two main types based on hardness: softness and hardness. Softness refers to low-density extruded polystyrene foam, primarily used for lightweight applications, insulation, and cushioning. It offers excellent thermal properties and is easy to handle. Hardness, on the other hand, involves higher-density variants designed for structural support, load-bearing applications, and enhanced durability. Both types serve critical roles in construction, geoengineering, and environmental projects, catering to different performance and insulation needs.</p></p>
