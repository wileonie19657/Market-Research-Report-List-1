<p><p>An Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometer (OFDR) is an advanced optical measurement tool employed primarily in the characterization and analysis of fiber optic systems. It operates by using a tunable laser to generate frequency-swept light, allowing for precise measurements of backscattered light from imperfections or changes in the fiber. This technology is integral in applications such as fiber optic sensing, telecom network maintenance, and structural health monitoring.</p><p>The Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometer Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-speed communication networks and advancements in fiber optic technologies.
