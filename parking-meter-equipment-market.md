<p><p>The parking meter equipment market encompasses two primary types: single-space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single-space meters are standalone devices that serve individual parking spots, providing a simple and straightforward payment solution for users. In contrast, multi-space kiosks serve multiple parking spaces, allowing users to pay for several spots at once, often offering advanced features like credit card payment, digital display, and detailed transaction records. Both types enhance efficiency, streamline payment processes, and support effective parking management.</p></p>
