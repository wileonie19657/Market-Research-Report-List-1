<p><p>The cold rolled low carbon steel drum barrel market consists of two primary types: open head steel drums and closed head steel drums. Open head drums feature a removable lid, allowing easy access for filling and cleaning, making them ideal for products requiring frequent access. Closed head drums, on the other hand, have sealed tops and are suitable for storing hazardous materials and liquids, offering better protection against leaks. Both types are widely used in various industries for safe and efficient storage.</p></p>
