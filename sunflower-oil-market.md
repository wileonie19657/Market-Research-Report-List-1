<p><p>The sunflower oil market comprises three main types: linoleic oil, mid-oleic oil, and high-oleic oil. Linoleic oil, rich in polyunsaturated fats, is preferred for its health benefits and commonly used in cooking and food products. Mid-oleic oil strikes a balance between monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats, offering improved oxidative stability while maintaining health benefits. High-oleic oil has elevated monounsaturated fat content, making it ideal for frying and extended shelf life, appealing to both health-conscious consumers and food manufacturers.</p></p>
