<p><ul><li>Hardware-based AV-over-IP</li><li>Software-based AV-over-IP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AV-over-IP market consists of two primary types: hardware-based and software-based solutions. Hardware-based AV-over-IP involves dedicated devices like encoders, decoders, and switches that facilitate audio-visual signal transmission over IP networks. This approach offers reliability and often better performance for large installations. Conversely, software-based AV-over-IP utilizes applications that run on standard computers or servers to manage and distribute AV content. This flexibility allows for ease of integration and scalability, suitable for diverse environments and budgets.</p></p>
