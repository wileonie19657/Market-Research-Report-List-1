<p><p>The GPS Auto Monitoring System market features various transmission types, primarily the Wired Transmission System and Dial Transmission Mode System. The Wired Transmission System relies on physical connections, ensuring a stable and uninterrupted data flow, ideal for fleet tracking in urban environments. Conversely, the Dial Transmission Mode System utilizes telephone lines to transmit data, providing a more flexible option for areas with limited internet access. Both systems cater to different user needs, enhancing asset tracking and security.</p></p>
