<p><ul><li>Neutral Type</li><li>Alkalinity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiammonia turbine oil market features two primary types: neutral type and alkalinity type. Neutral type oils are formulated to minimize acid formation, thus protecting turbine components from corrosion and degradation. They are suitable for environments with limited ammonia presence. Alkalinity type oils, on the other hand, contain additives that neutralize acids, providing enhanced protection in high ammonia conditions. This distinction allows industries to choose the appropriate oil type based on their operational conditions and compatibility with various turbine systems.</p></p>
