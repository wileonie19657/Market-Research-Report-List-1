<p><strong>The Hydrogen Electrolyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen electrolyser market comprises two main types: traditional alkaline electrolysers and PEM (proton exchange membrane) electrolysers. Traditional alkaline electrolysers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte, are cost-effective, and offer robust operation, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, PEM electrolysers use a solid polymer electrolyte, allowing for higher efficiency and faster response times; they are ideal for integration with renewable energy sources. Both technologies contribute significantly to the growing hydrogen economy by enabling sustainable hydrogen production.</p></p>
