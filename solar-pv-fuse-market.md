<p><p>The Solar PV Fuse market includes several types tailored for protecting photovoltaic systems. Midget fuses, known for their compact size, are used in smaller circuits. Class R fuses provide reliable overcurrent protection and are designed for reduced physical space. Class J fuses offer higher interrupting ratings and are ideal for equipment requiring robust protection. NH or square body style fuses are larger, commonly used in industrial applications, providing high current protection and robust performance in solar installations.</p></p>
