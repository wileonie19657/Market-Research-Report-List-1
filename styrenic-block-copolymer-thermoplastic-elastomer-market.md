<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymer Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPSPs) are divided into three main types: Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS), and Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS). SBS features good oil and weather resistance, making it suitable for applications like adhesives and sealants. SEBS offers enhanced thermal and UV stability, often used in consumer goods and automotive parts. SIS provides excellent elasticity and is commonly utilized in films and adhesives. Together, these materials cater to diverse industries, delivering flexibility and durability.</p></p>
