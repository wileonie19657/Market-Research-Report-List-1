<p><p>Needle biopsy is a minimally invasive procedure used to obtain tissue samples from various organs for diagnostic purposes. <p><strong>The Needle Biopsy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fine-Needle Aspiration Biopsy</li><li>Core Needle Biopsy</li><li>Vacuum-Assisted Biopsy</li><li>Image-Guided Biopsy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The needle biopsy market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Fine-Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNAB)** uses a thin needle to extract cellular material for examination, ideal for superficial lesions. </p><p>2. **Core Needle Biopsy** involves a larger needle to obtain tissue samples, providing more comprehensive information about the lesion. </p><p>3. **Vacuum-Assisted Biopsy** employs suction to collect multiple tissue samples, offering efficiency for larger areas. </p><p>4. **Image-Guided Biopsy** utilizes imaging techniques, like ultrasound or MRI, to precisely locate and sample lesions, enhancing accuracy in pathological assessment.</p></p>
