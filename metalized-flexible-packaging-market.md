<p><strong>The Metalized Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mono Extruded Structures</li><li>Insulated Structures</li><li>Laminated Structures</li><li>Co-Extruded Structures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metalized flexible packaging market comprises various types, including mono extruded structures, insulated structures, laminated structures, and co-extruded structures. Mono extruded structures involve single-layer films that provide moisture and oxygen barriers. Insulated structures enhance thermal protection, ideal for temperature-sensitive products. Laminated structures combine multiple layers for superior strength and barrier properties. Co-extruded structures utilize multiple polymer layers in a single process, offering cost-effective solutions with customized properties. Together, these types cater to diverse packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
