<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Electrostatic Chucks (ESC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are essential for holding wafers securely during processing in semiconductor manufacturing. The market is primarily divided into two types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb type ESCs utilize electrostatic forces generated by charged plates, providing strong wafer grip. In contrast, JR type ESCs operate through the electrostatic attraction between a dielectric surface and a conductive wafer, offering improved temperature management and uniformity. Both types are critical for enhancing efficiency and precision in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
