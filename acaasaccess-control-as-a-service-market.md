<p><p>Access Control as a Service (ACaaS) provides organizations with cloud-based access management solutions. The market is primarily divided into two types: Hosted and Managed. </p><p>In the Hosted model, the service provider maintains the infrastructure and software, but the user is responsible for managing their access policies. Conversely, the Managed market offers a more comprehensive solution where the provider oversees not only the infrastructure but also the access policies and monitoring, ensuring a seamless and secure experience for users.</p></p>
