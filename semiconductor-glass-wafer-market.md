<p><p>The semiconductor glass wafer market includes types like borosilicate glass, quartz, and fused silica, each serving distinct roles in semiconductor manufacturing. Borosilicate glass offers thermal resistance and durability, ideal for protective layers and substrates. Quartz provides high purity and thermal stability, suitable for high-temperature processes. Fused silica excels in optical clarity and low thermal expansion, making it perfect for precision applications like photolithography. These materials cater to diverse semiconductor needs, ensuring performance, reliability, and efficiency in advanced technologies.</p></p>
