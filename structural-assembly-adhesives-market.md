<p><p>The structural assembly adhesives market is characterized by two main types: paste and tape. Paste adhesives, often used for bonding, offer strong adhesion and are ideal for a variety of materials, providing flexibility in application. They are commonly utilized in manufacturing, automotive, and construction sectors. Tape adhesives, on the other hand, feature pre-applied adhesive layers for quick and convenient bonding. They ensure clean application with precise placement and are increasingly popular in electronics and lightweight assembly applications, enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
