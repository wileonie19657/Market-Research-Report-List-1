<p><strong>The Mechanical Torque Limiter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Torque Limiter</li><li>Friction Torque Limiter</li><li>Pneumatic Torque Limiter</li><li>Push/Pull Torque Limiter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical torque limiter market features various types, including ball, friction, pneumatic, and push/pull torque limiters. Ball torque limiters use balls to slip when torque exceeds a preset level, protecting machinery. Friction torque limiters rely on friction plates to disengage when threshold torque is reached. Pneumatic torque limiters utilize air pressure to adjust torque limits dynamically. Push/pull torque limiters operate by physically engaging or disengaging components based on torque conditions, ensuring safe and efficient machinery operation across various industries.</p></p>
