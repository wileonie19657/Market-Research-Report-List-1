<p><strong>The Motor Vehicle Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Third Party Only</li><li>Third Party Fire & Theft Insurance</li><li>Comprehensive Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motor vehicle insurance encompasses three primary market types. Third Party Only insurance covers liability for damages to other people and their property in an accident but does not cover the policyholder's own vehicle. Third Party Fire & Theft Insurance provides the same liability coverage as Third Party Only, but also includes protection against fire damage and theft of the insured vehicle. Comprehensive Insurance offers extensive coverage, including damages to the policyholder's vehicle from accidents, theft, fire, and other incidents, providing the highest level of protection.</p></p>
