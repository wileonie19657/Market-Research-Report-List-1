<p><p>MEMS optical switches are essential components in optical networks, enabling efficient signal routing. The MEMS single-mode optical switches are designed for applications requiring high-speed data transmission over long distances, offering low loss and minimal signal degradation. In contrast, MEMS multimode optical switches are suited for shorter distances and applications where multiple light paths are necessary, providing versatility in managing varied data rates. Both technologies enhance network performance, reliability, and scalability in telecommunications and data center environments.</p></p>
