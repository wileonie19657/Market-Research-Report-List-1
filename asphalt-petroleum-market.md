<p><p>The asphalt market comprises primarily two types: paving grade petroleum asphalt and industrial grade petroleum asphalt. Paving grade asphalt is designed specifically for road construction and maintenance, featuring appropriate viscosity and temperature characteristics for durability and performance. On the other hand, industrial grade petroleum asphalt is used in various applications, including roofing, waterproofing, and sealants, where specialized properties like flexibility and adhesion are required. Both types are derived from crude oil refining, catering to distinct industry needs.</p></p>
