<p><strong>The Industrial Food Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Crew</li><li>Twin Crew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial food extruder market is divided into two main types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders utilize a single rotating screw to process food materials, making them ideal for straightforward applications like snack production. In contrast, twin screw extruders feature two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, temperature control, and the ability to process a wider range of ingredients, making them suitable for complex formulations such as extruded cereals and pet food.</p></p>
