The market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Voltage Switch Type SPD**: Engages during over-voltage conditions to redirect excess energy.</p><p>2. **Pressure Limiting Type SPD**: Limits voltage levels by diverting surges, ensuring equipment safety.</p><p>3. **Combination Type SPD**: Integrates features of both voltage switch and pressure limiting types for comprehensive protection. </p><p>These varying SPDs offer tailored solutions to protect sensitive electronics across different applications.</p></p>
