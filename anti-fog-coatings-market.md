<p><strong>The Anti-Fog Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foggy Guard Coating (FGC)</li><li>Defog Coating (DFC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Fog Coatings market comprises two primary types: Foggy Guard Coating (FGC) and Defog Coating (DFC). FGC is designed to prevent condensation on surfaces, ensuring clarity in humid or temperature-variable environments. DFC, on the other hand, actively defogs surfaces by promoting the uniform spread of moisture, reducing fog formation. Both coatings are widely used in eyewear, automotive, and safety equipment, enhancing visibility and safety in various applications, especially where vision clarity is critical.</p></p>
