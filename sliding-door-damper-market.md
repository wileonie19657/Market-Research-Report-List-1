<p><p>The sliding door damper market consists of three main types: mechanical, pneumatic, and hydraulic dampers. Mechanical dampers use physical components to control door movement and reduce noise, while pneumatic dampers utilize air pressure to achieve smooth operation and cushioning effects. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to various applications in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
