<p><p>Small Ale market types include limit fermentation and dealcoholization methods. Limit fermentation involves controlling yeast activity to produce lower alcohol content during brewing, resulting in a flavorful, low-alcohol beverage. In contrast, dealcoholization methods remove alcohol from fully fermented beer, allowing for the retention of traditional flavors while achieving a non-alcoholic end product. Both techniques cater to consumers seeking lighter options, emphasizing taste and versatility while promoting healthier drinking habits in the growing craft beer segment.</p></p>
