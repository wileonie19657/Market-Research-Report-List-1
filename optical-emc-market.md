<p><strong>The Optical EMC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Optical EMC</li><li>Liquid Optical EMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical EMC (Electromagnetic Compatibility) market is divided into two primary types: solid optical EMC and liquid optical EMC. Solid optical EMC encompasses materials and devices that provide electromagnetic shielding in solid forms, often used in connectors and circuit boards. In contrast, liquid optical EMC involves liquid materials that offer similar shielding properties, typically utilized in coatings and potting compounds. Both types are essential for reducing electromagnetic interference and ensuring the reliable performance of electronic devices across various industries.</p></p>
