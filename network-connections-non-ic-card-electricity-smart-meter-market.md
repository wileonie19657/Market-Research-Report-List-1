<p><p>The Network Connections Non-IC Card Electricity Smart Meter market is categorized into single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase meters are typically used in residential settings, measuring energy consumption through one alternating current path, thus suitable for lower power requirements. In contrast, three-phase meters serve industrial and commercial applications, utilizing three alternating current paths for higher power loads, improving efficiency and load balancing. Each type is essential for effective energy management and billing in diverse electrical networks.</p></p>
