<p><ul><li>Single-Tube Shock Absorbers</li><li>Twin-Tube Shock Absorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Single-Tube Shock Absorber** features a single cylinder with a piston separating oil and gas, offering better heat dissipation and performance under high stress, ideal for heavy-duty or performance vehicles. The **Twin-Tube Shock Absorber** has an inner and outer tube, with oil and gas separated, providing smoother rides and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in standard passenger vehicles. Both types aim to dampen vibrations and enhance vehicle stability, but differ in design, performance, and application suitability.</p></p>
