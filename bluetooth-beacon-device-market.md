<p><p>Bluetooth beacon devices are categorized into three main types: iBeacon, Eddystone, and AltBeacon. iBeacon, developed by Apple, primarily focuses on proximity marketing and indoor positioning. Eddystone, created by Google, supports multiple protocols, enhancing versatility and allows for web-based interactions. AltBeacon, an open-source alternative, aims to ensure compatibility across various platforms and uses. Each type serves distinct purposes, enabling businesses to engage customers through location-based services, data collection, and personalized experiences in various environments.</p></p>
