<p><p>The anti-rust rolling oil market comprises two main types: cold rolling oil and hot rolling oil. Cold rolling oil is used in low-temperature metalworking processes, providing lubrication and anti-corrosion properties during the rolling of metals. It enhances surface finish and extends tool life. In contrast, hot rolling oil is designed for high-temperature applications, offering thermal stability and effective cooling during the shaping of metals. Both types play crucial roles in improving efficiency and preventing oxidation in metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
