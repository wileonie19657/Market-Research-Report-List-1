<p><p>The Endoscopic Ultrasound (EUS) needles market consists primarily of Aspiration Needles (EUS-FNA) and Biopsy Needles (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA is designed for fine-needle aspiration, allowing for the extraction of cytology samples from lesions and cysts for diagnostic purposes. In contrast, EUS-FNB offers larger gauge needles for obtaining core tissue samples, which are essential for histological analysis. Both needle types play a critical role in minimally invasive procedures, enhancing the accuracy and effectiveness of gastrointestinal diagnostics and treatments.</p></p>
