<p><p>The commercial solar battery market consists of various types, including lead-acid, lithium-ion (Li-ion), and saltwater batteries. Li-ion batteries are popular for their high energy density, efficiency, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for commercial applications. Saltwater batteries, a newer technology, use non-toxic materials and offer safety, sustainability, and good longevity, positioning them as an eco-friendly alternative for solar energy storage.</p></p>
