<p><p>The wrist and hand MRI equipment market is segmented into three types based on magnetic field strength. Low Field MRI utilizes magnetic fields of 0.2 to 0.5 Tesla, offering portability and reduced costs but lower resolution images. Mid-Field MRI operates at 0.5 to 1.0 Tesla, balancing image quality and patient comfort. High Field MRI, exceeding 1.5 Tesla, provides superior image clarity and resolution, making it ideal for detailed evaluation of wrist and hand conditions, though at a higher cost and less accessibility.</p></p>
