<p><p>Power Line Carrier (PLC) systems are categorized into two main market types: broadband and narrowband. Broadband PLC systems facilitate high-speed data transmission over power lines, supporting applications like internet access and multimedia services. In contrast, narrowband PLC systems focus on low-speed communication, typically used for applications such as smart metering and remote monitoring. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements, data rate needs, and the existing power line infrastructure's capabilities.</p></p>
