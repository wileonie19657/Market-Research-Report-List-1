<p><strong>The Gas Turbine Electrical Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT)</li><li>Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas turbine electrical power generation market primarily consists of two types: Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT) and Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT). OCGT plants operate by generating electricity through a single cycle, using natural gas as fuel, making them suitable for peaking power and rapid response scenarios. In contrast, CCGT systems utilize both gas and steam turbines in a combined cycle, enhancing efficiency and output by recycling exhaust heat, making them ideal for base load and intermediate power generation.</p></p>
