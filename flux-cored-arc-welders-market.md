<p><p>Flux Cored Arc Welders (FCAW) are specialized welding machines that utilize a tubular wire filled with flux, which generates shielding gas when heated during the welding process. This method is particularly advantageous for its efficiency, versatility in outdoor applications, and ability to weld thick materials.
