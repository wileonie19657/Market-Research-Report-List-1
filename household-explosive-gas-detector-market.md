<p><p>The Household Explosive Gas Detector market comprises two primary types: Plug-in Type and Portable Type. Plug-in Type detectors are designed to be installed in fixed locations, usually near potential gas sources, and require an electrical outlet for operation. They provide continuous monitoring and typically feature alarms for gas detection. Portable Type detectors, on the other hand, are battery-operated and designed for mobility, allowing users to monitor gas levels in various locations. Both types are essential for enhancing household safety by detecting hazardous gas leaks.</p></p>
