<p><strong>The Replacement Rear View Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The replacement rear view mirrors market is primarily segmented into CCD camera and CMOS camera types. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them ideal for enhanced visibility in challenging conditions. In contrast, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras offer lower power consumption, compact size, and faster processing speeds, making them increasingly popular for modern vehicles. Both types improve safety by providing drivers with clearer rear views and reducing blind spots.</p></p>
