<p><p>The Diamond Substrate market includes two primary types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and High-Pressure High-Temperature (HPHT) substrates. CVD Diamond Substrates are synthesized by depositing carbon atoms onto a surface in a controlled environment, producing high-purity, high-performance diamonds ideal for electronics, optics, and thermal management. HPHT Diamond Substrates are created under extreme pressure and temperature, mimicking natural diamond formation, and are widely used in industrial applications like cutting tools and abrasives due to their durability and hardness. Both types cater to specialized industrial and technological needs.</p></p>
