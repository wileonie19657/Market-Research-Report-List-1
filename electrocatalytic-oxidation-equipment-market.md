<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Indirect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrocatalytic oxidation equipment market is divided into two main types: direct and indirect. Direct electrocatalytic oxidation involves directly applying an electric current to facilitate the oxidation of contaminants at the anode, promoting chemical reactions efficiently. In contrast, indirect electrocatalytic oxidation employs an intermediary process, often involving the generation of reactive species that interact with the contaminants. Both methods aim to enhance the oxidation process, but they differ in their operational mechanisms and energy efficiency.</p></p>
