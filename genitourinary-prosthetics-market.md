Titanium prosthetics are known for their strength and durability, often utilized in more invasive procedures due to their resistance to corrosion. Apatite, a bioactive ceramic material, promotes bone integration and healing, making it a preferred choice for certain surgical applications. Each type serves specific patient needs and enhances quality of life.</p></p>
