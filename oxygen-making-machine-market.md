<p><p>The oxygen-making machine market is primarily segmented into three types: Molecular Sieve, Oxygen-rich Membrane, and Others. Molecular sieve technology utilizes zeolites to selectively adsorb nitrogen from air, producing high-purity oxygen. Oxygen-rich membrane systems employ selective permeability, allowing oxygen to pass through while filtering out other gases. The "Others" category includes alternative methods like cryogenic distillation and pressure swing adsorption, catering to various industrial applications by providing customized oxygen generation solutions to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
