<p><p>The enteric empty capsules market is primarily categorized into two types: Gelatin and HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose). Gelatin capsules, derived from animal sources, offer excellent bioavailability and are widely used for their rapid dissolution in the intestine. In contrast, HPMC capsules are vegetarian, derived from cellulose, and are favored for their stability and suitability for various dietary preferences. Both types cater to pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries, ensuring targeted delivery of active ingredients while enhancing product formulation flexibility.</p></p>
